What The House Can Do

Every capability runs privately on your hardware. Not one word, image, or camera frame leaves your network.

  • Person, vehicle, pet, package detection — real time on all cameras
  • Facial recognition — identifies known guests by face at entry
  • License plate recognition — logs known and unknown vehicles
  • All footage stored locally — no cloud, no subscription required

Animated Avatar System

  • Theatrical curtain reveal — velvet curtains open on wake word
  • Real-time lip sync — 30fps mouth movement via MuseTalk 1.5
  • Emotion-driven expressions — pleased, thoughtful, attentive, surprised

  • 16 custom wake words — each persona wakes to their own name
