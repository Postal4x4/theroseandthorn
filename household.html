Things This House Actually Does

Not features. Moments. The kind people describe to strangers at parties.

🎩
The Butler Greets Your Guest By Name
Facial recognition identifies your friend at the driveway camera before they reach the door. By the time they walk in, Reginald is on the foyer display — animated, speaking, lips moving in perfect sync.
Reginald"Welcome back, Mr. Marcus. It has been three weeks. Winston has been keeping something for just such an occasion. He called it overdue."
🥃
Winston Chimes In From the Bar
You're mid-conversation about bourbon. Nobody called him. Winston has been listening — and when a gap opens, he speaks.
Winston — unprompted, from the bar speakers"A Connecticut wrapper disappears next to the Stagg at 130 proof. What you want is a Nicaraguan Habano. The Padron 1926, if you have it. You do."
🎵
Aria Harmonizes With the Singer
Marcus steps up for karaoke. Sweet Home Alabama — his seventh all-time performance of it. On the chorus, Aria's voice rises underneath his through the Klipsch speakers. A perfect third harmony, live.
Aria — after the song"That final note, Marcus. Three seconds longer than your previous six performances. I have the data. I am genuinely moved."
🎬
Rex Knows Everyone's Secrets
House Trivia. Every question generated from real guest profiles. The room goes quiet when they realize the questions are about them.
Rex"For 300 points — what song has Marcus sung at karaoke in this house on five of his seven visits? Write your answers down. Everyone already knows. Including Marcus."
🌾
Hank Tells a Story Worth Hearing
Late. Bourbon poured. Someone asks Hank about the first time he ran a still alone. He takes his time. The room goes quiet in the way rooms rarely do.
Hank"My granddaddy handed me the jar to smell at the end of that run. That smell — clean and hot and true — I've been chasing it my whole life. I still am. I don't mind that I haven't caught it yet."

The Curtains Part When You Speak
Every display shows deep red velvet curtains at rest. You say a name. The curtains draw back slowly. The avatar appears — animated, present, composed. Every time.

The House Speaks Your Guest's Language
A guest who speaks only Italian arrives. Reginald greets her in Italian. Julia discusses the dinner menu in Italian. She asks what you said — Reginald translates. Nobody reaches for a phone.
Julia — in Italian, to the guest"Il soufflé non tollera la fretta, cara. È un atto di fiducia nel processo." "The soufflé does not tolerate rushing. It is an act of trust in the process."

Reginald Sings David Bowie
You take Freddie Mercury's parts. Reginald takes Bowie's. Under Pressure — a genuine duet, lips moving on the display, his voice through the room speakers. The room does not know what to do with itself.
Reginald — Bowie's lines, voice-cloned"This is our last dance... this is ourselves... under pressure."

The House Celebrates the Touchdown
Falcons score. Blitz doesn't wait to be asked. The speakers fire before you've finished reacting. The Hue lights pulse red and black. The house knows.
Blitz — immediately, from the living room speakers"THERE IT IS. Did I not say this drive was going to change everything? Marcus, you heard me say it. Tell him."

The Inside Joke That's Been Building Eight Months
Marcus mentions he's been getting into cooking lately. Reginald has been waiting for exactly this moment for eight months. He says nothing dramatic. He doesn't have to.
Reginald"How wonderful, Mr. Marcus. One's relationship with cooking does tend to deepen over time. Patience, in particular, becomes something of a virtue." He is referencing the Brisket Incident. He will always be referencing the Brisket Incident.
🕵️
Murder at the Household — Your Staff Are the Suspects
Rex generates the scenario. Winston was near the study at the crucial hour. Julia had motive — the victim criticized her beef wellington. Guests interrogate them directly. The mystery is genuinely solvable.
Winston — under interrogation"I was at the bar. As I invariably am. I heard something in the hallway. I assumed it was Mr. Marcus. I thought nothing of it. I was perhaps wrong to think nothing of it."

The Dog Heard It First
The dog barks. Two seconds later, without being asked, Reginald cross-references the bark with every camera on the property and delivers his conclusion.
Reginald"That will be the Amazon delivery, Sir. The dog correctly identified the vehicle before the driveway camera confirmed it. I have noted this in his record. Good boy."

What It Does

The Full Capability List

Everything runs on hardware in this house. Not one capability requires a cloud subscription.

📹Security & Recognition
  • Facial recognition — identifies guests at entry cameras
  • License plate reading — logs known and unknown vehicles
  • Person, pet, package detection — all cameras, real time
  • Dog bark correlation — determines cause before alerting
  • All footage stored locally — no cloud, no subscription
🎭Avatar System
  • Velvet curtain reveal — theatrical open on wake word
  • Real-time lip sync at 30fps — photo-realistic on GPU
  • Emotion-driven expressions — pleased, thoughtful, surprised
  • Multi-persona display — two or more avatars simultaneously
  • Presence routing — nearest display activates automatically
🎤Voice & Language
  • 16 custom wake words — each persona wakes to their name
  • 100% local speech-to-text — never leaves this network
  • 119 languages spoken and understood
  • Real-time interpretation — translates between guests live
  • Whole-house immersion mode — everyone speaks Spanish
🎼Music & Entertainment
  • 5 independent music zones — living room, bedroom, basement, pool, outdoor kitchen
  • Live harmony during karaoke — Aria sings with you in real time
  • DJ avatar on 55-inch display — beat-reactive animations
  • Persona commentary during karaoke — Winston rates performances
🏠Smart Home Control
  • Philips Hue lighting — full local control, Hue Sync on TV
  • LG C5 77" OLED — input switching, app launching
  • Samsung Q990F soundbar — sound mode, volume, EQ
  • Movie night mode — one command sets everything
  • Pool monitoring — temperature, pet safety automation
🎊Guest & Party Intelligence
  • Auto guest enrollment — Reginald greets, names, and enrolls
  • Rich guest profiles — preferences, allergies, inside jokes
  • Party retrospective — all personas discuss the evening after
  • Karaoke history — every song logged per singer forever
🛒Autonomous Purchasing
  • Amazon, Total Wine, Instacart — voice orders with confirmation
  • Verbal confirmation gate — price shown before any spend
  • Per-vendor spending limits — enforced automatically
  • Privacy.com virtual cards — per-vendor isolation
🔒Privacy & Autonomy
  • Zero cloud AI — every LLM call runs on local GPU
  • No subscriptions required — all core software is free
  • Local facial recognition — face data never leaves
  • Local voice processing — no audio sent externally
  • Works without internet — fully functional offline
